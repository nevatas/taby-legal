# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 28, 2026

**Taby** ("the App") is developed and maintained by THE COOL COMPANY ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

All notes, tabs, images, and settings you create in the App are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

## Data Storage

- All content is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and UserDefaults.
- We do not operate servers or databases that store your data.
- We do not use cloud synchronization services (such as iCloud or any third-party cloud).

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

- Analytics or tracking tools
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Social media SDKs

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties, because we do not collect any.

## Photos and Camera

The App allows you to attach photos from your photo library or camera. These images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
